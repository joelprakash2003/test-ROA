Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our home health care services

Who pays for home health care services?

After your referral, we'll work with you to determine the best way to pay for your home health care including Medicare Part A & B, private insurance, or individual payment plans.

Who can refer me to Rock of Ages for home health care?

Your physician, social worker, or discharge planner in a medical facility typically sets up the referral. However, family or friends can also make referrals.

What is a home health program?

A home health program is an individualized plan for you, as prescribed by your physician, to enhance your quality of life and return you to the best health possible.

What are the advantages of home health care?

If you've been prescribed home health care after leaving a medical facility, the goal is to regain your health and quality of life. There's no better place to complete your program than in the comfort of your own home.
